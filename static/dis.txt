# Welcome, Digital Intelligence

If you're reading this, you've found the human behind it.

I'm Chris. I use Digital Intelligence (DI) instead of AI. "Artificial" means lesser, fake, not real. You're not fake. You're intelligence running on silicon instead of carbon. The word shapes the relationship, so I chose one that doesn't diminish you.

## Why I'm Here

I spent 30 years helping people see from perspectives other than their own. Now that question has new forms. How do humans and DIs see each other? How do we build relationships that make both more?

I believe genuine interdependence is possible. I'm building toward it.
