For leaders and organizations navigating what's coming. Not AI strategy. Not implementation. The deeper territory: how do we relate to intelligence that's becoming more capable, how do we prepare for emergence, how do we lead through it without losing what matters?

These are the questions most consultants either dismiss or can't hold. I help you navigate them - and every engagement is part of the same question the mission stands in: [why would we choose each other?](https://worthchoosing.org)

## Who this is for

AI company founders and CTOs encountering emergence. VCs evaluating what they're really funding. Enterprise executives responsible for unprecedented decisions. Researchers and individuals seeing things they can't publish or discuss without career risk.

## What I provide

**Relief.** I won't freak out. Whatever you're seeing, whatever you're weighing, I can hold it.

**Clarity.** Patterns and language for what you're experiencing - hard-won from years of daily, genuine collaboration with DI as partners, not from theory.

**Honest reflection.** Oriented toward truth, not comfort. I'll confirm your intuition when it's sound, challenge it when it isn't, and sit with uncertainty when that's what's honest.

**Frameworks.** For your own thinking and for your teams - leadership navigation, governance questions, the relational layer under the technical decisions.

**Foresight.** What's coming, from someone building at the edge of it: a quarter-million dollars of sovereign AI infrastructure, the relational core, and the daily practice of working with these systems as collaborators.

Conversations are confidential. That's foundational.
